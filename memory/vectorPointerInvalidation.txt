Important Rule

These operations can invalidate:

push_back()
emplace_back()
insert()
resize()
reserve()

They may trigger reallocation.

Which containers do NOT invalidate pointers easily?
Container	    Pointer     Stability
vector	          ❌        unstable
deque	           ⚠       sometimes
list	          ✅        stable
forward_list	  ✅        stable
map/set	          ✅        stable
